About Us

Dhristhi is a startup in India that aims to bridge the gap between rural artisans and their potential customers, helping them expand their market reach nationwide. Our core philosophy revolves around empowering rural artisans to find potential markets for their creativity and innovation, all with a distinctive desi touch.

Our core philosophy revolves around empowering rural artisans to find their potential markets for their creativity and innovation, all with a distinctive Desi touch .We also provide a safe and secure creator space and market , offer employment opportunities, and extend support to improve the overall quality of life for those in need.

With a dedicated team of over 45 rural artisans [heritage hustlers] spread across states Bihar , Assam , Arunachal Pradesh, Manipal, Meghalaya, Manipur , Mizoram and Nagaland. We are committed to bring recognition to the rural artisans and leave a long lasting impact on India in the category of handicrafts .
